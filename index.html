UI/UX Group

"UX design is about understanding the overall journey of your users and turning it into a product. The role of a user experience designer is to understand the customer journey. That means understanding the target audience, interviewing customers, defining user flows, and conducting user testing".

UI design is the process of transforming wireframes into a polished graphical user interface. a UI designer’s job begins where a UX designer’s job ends—at the prototyping stage. They take the wireframes and add visual design to make them more usable, aesthetically appealing, and optimized for different screen sizes.
